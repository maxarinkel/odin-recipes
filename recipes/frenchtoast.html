French Toast

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanill, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golde, 3 to 4 minutes per side
  6. Serve hot
