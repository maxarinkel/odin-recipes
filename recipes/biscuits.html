Biscuits

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Whisk dry ingredients together in large bowl
  3. Add buttermilk and stir gently
  4. Put dough onto a floured surface and pat into a rectangle
  5. Fold dough three times, then roll on a floured surface to about 1/2 thickness
  6. Use a 2 1/2 inch round cutter to cut biscuits
  7. Bake until golden brown, about 15 minutes
